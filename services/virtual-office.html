FAQ

Common Questions

How is this different from Zoom or Slack?
Zoom and Slack improve how meetings and messages work in the old way. Your virtual office replaces the office itself — your team has avatars, can walk around, pop into rooms, and collaborate spontaneously. No scheduling, no links, no missed messages.
Do we need to download anything?
The virtual office works in the browser. Team members can also download a desktop app for the best experience. Nothing complicated — we handle the setup and onboarding for your entire team.
How does per-user pricing work?
You pay $20 per user per month. 3 users = $60/month, 5 users = $100/month. For teams of 5 or more, contact us for a custom bulk rate.
Can I use it for a registered LLP or Pvt Ltd?
Yes. The professional business address is GST-ready and suitable for company registrations. We provide the necessary documentation for compliance.
Is voice and video secure?
Yes. All voice and video is encrypted with TLS and is not stored on any server. You can also lock individual meeting rooms for complete privacy.
How long does setup take?
2–3 business days after payment. We configure everything, set up your office layout, onboard your team, and make sure everyone knows how to use it before we hand it over.

FAQ

Common Questions

How many emails can I send per day?
Up to 300 emails per day using our Brevo-powered delivery infrastructure. This is ideal for targeted campaigns to warm or cold lists.
Is there a free trial?
No. We don't offer a free trial. However, you can view the live tool at app.tssolutionsllp.com and contact us if you have questions before subscribing.
Do I need any technical knowledge?
No. The tool is designed to be simple and straightforward. We also provide onboarding support to get you started.
Can I import my existing contact list?
Yes. You can import contacts via CSV file. The tool supports bulk uploads with a preview before confirming.
What happens to my contacts if I cancel?
You can export your contact list at any time before cancelling. We do not retain your data after account closure.

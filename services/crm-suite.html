FAQ

Common Questions

What is a Whitelabel CRM?
A whitelabel CRM is branded with your company name, logo, and colors instead of ours. Your team and clients see only your brand — perfect for agencies and larger businesses.
How long does CRM setup take?
Initial setup takes 3–7 business days depending on complexity. We also provide onboarding training for your team.
Can I migrate my existing data?
Yes. We can import your existing contacts and data from spreadsheets, other CRMs, or CSV files.
Is there a limit on contacts?
No hard limit. Both plans accommodate growing contact databases. We'll discuss your needs during consultation.
What currency are your prices in?
All prices are in USD. We accept international payments via bank transfer, Wise, PayPal, and other methods.

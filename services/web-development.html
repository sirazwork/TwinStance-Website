FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build my website?
Standard websites are delivered within 24–48 hours after we receive all your content and requirements. Larger or more complex sites may take slightly longer.
Do I need to provide content?
Ideally yes — your logo, photos, and text. But if you don't have content ready, we can write basic content for you and use placeholder images to get started.
What if I want changes after delivery?
You get free support after delivery for minor changes (1 month on Starter, 3 months on Business, 6 months on Premium). Major redesigns are quoted separately.
Will my website rank on Google?
We build every website with SEO best practices. However, ranking takes time and depends on competition. For active SEO, check our SEO Services.
What is the setup fee?
The setup fee covers domain configuration, hosting setup, and initial customization. The exact amount is discussed during your free consultation based on your requirements.
What currency are your prices in?
All prices are in USD. We accept international payments via bank transfer, Wise, PayPal, and other methods discussed during consultation.

FAQ

Common Questions

What is the minimum ad budget I need?
We recommend a minimum ad spend of $150–$300/month for Google Ads to see meaningful results. For Facebook Ads, $100–$200/month is a good starting point. Your actual budget depends on your industry and goals — we'll advise during consultation.
Do you guarantee results?
We guarantee professional management and continuous optimization. Results depend on your industry, competition, and budget. We'll set realistic expectations during your consultation.
Who pays Google and Facebook directly?
You do. Your ad budget goes directly from your card or account to the ad platform. We only charge our management fee of $59/month separately.
How soon will my ads go live?
We set up and launch your campaigns within 3–5 business days after receiving your brief and payment.
What currency are your prices in?
All prices are in USD. We accept international payments via bank transfer, Wise, PayPal, and other methods.

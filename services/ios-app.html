FAQ

Common Questions

How long does it take to build an iOS app?
A simple app takes 4–8 weeks. A complex app with many features can take 3–6 months. We'll give you a realistic timeline during your consultation.
Do I own the source code?
Yes. Once full payment is made, you own all the source code and assets.
Can you update the app after launch?
Yes. We offer ongoing maintenance and update packages. We can also train you to make minor updates yourself.
Do I need a Apple App Store developer account?
Yes, you'll need a Apple App Store developer account (one-time fee of $99 per year). We'll guide you through setting it up.

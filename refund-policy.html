3. Exceptions — When We May Consider a Refund

While our general policy is no refunds, we may consider exceptions in the following very specific circumstances:

  • Duplicate payment — if you were charged twice for the same service due to a technical error

✅ Refund requests under the above exceptions must be submitted within 7 days of the transaction date to admin@tssolutionsllp.com with proof of payment.

FAQ

Common Questions

Who is this for?
This is for digital agencies, marketing consultants, entrepreneurs, and businesses who want to offer email marketing as a product to their own clients. You buy it once and can charge your users monthly recurring fees.
Do I need technical knowledge to manage it?
No. We handle all the technical setup and deployment. We also provide training and documentation so you can manage users and settings yourself. Ongoing technical maintenance is available as an optional add-on.
What are the ongoing costs after purchase?
You will need a Brevo account for email sending (free plan covers 300 emails/day, paid plans go higher), and hosting costs (typically ₹1,000 to ₹3,000/month on platforms like Render or Railway). There are no ongoing fees payable to TwinStance unless you opt for our support package.
Can I charge my own clients to use the platform?
Yes, absolutely. That is the whole point. You can set your own pricing and charge your clients whatever you like. The Business plan includes Razorpay integration so you can collect payments directly.
Can I add features or customize the platform further?
Yes. We offer custom development at our standard development rates. The Enterprise plan includes a budget for custom features as part of the package.
How long does setup take?
Standard deployment takes 5 to 7 business days from receipt of your branding assets and payment. The Enterprise plan with custom features may take 2 to 4 weeks.
